Addendum

This interactive story, "Life is Strange: Avagio Bay," is a captivating narrative experience crafted by Avagio Bay Studios. Dive into the intriguing world of Avagio Bay, where your decisions shape the unfolding story.

As you explore the town, attend classes, and encounter mysterious events, your choices will lead to unique consequences, unraveling the mysteries hidden within the narrative.

Immerse yourself in the beautifully illustrated scenes, each reflecting the essence of your chosen path. From the halls of Avagio Bay High to the artistic expressions in classes, your journey is filled with creativity and adventure.

This interactive story invites you to embark on unexpected quests, express your creativity through art, and uncover the secrets that lie beneath the surface of Avagio Bay.

Whether you're painting vibrant landscapes, conducting chemistry experiments, or facing hidden quests, your decisions will define your character's destiny.

Enjoy the rich storytelling, make choices that resonate with you, and discover the multitude of paths that Avagio Bay has to offer. Your adventure awaits!
